Numeric Sequence Generator

Instructions

  1. Select the type of sequence to be generated from the drop down
  2. Enter a whole number to decide the last digit of the series
  3. Click on the Generate Sequence
  4. The generated sequence will be shown below

Types of sequence

  • Entire Sequence: All the numbers from 0 till last digit is displayed
  • Odd Sequence: All the odd numbers from 1 till last digit is displayed
  • Even Sequence: All the even numbers from 0 till last digit is displayed
  • Fibonacci Sequence: Fibonacci series till last digit is displayed
  • CEZ Sequence: Same as the Entire Sequence with below replacements
    1. Multiples of 3 are replaced by 'C'
    2. Multiples of 5 are replaced by 'E'
    3. Multiples of 3 and 5 are replaced by 'Z'
